# 🛡️ InternShield – Fake Internship Detection System

InternShield is an AI-inspired internship scam detection platform designed to help students identify fake or suspicious internship offers before falling victim to scams.

The system analyzes internship details such as company information, email domains, registration fees, communication methods, salary offers, and scam-related keywords to generate a risk score and classify the internship as Safe, Suspicious, or High Risk.

---

# 🚀 Features

## 🔍 Internship Risk Analysis
- Analyze internship offers instantly
- Detect suspicious patterns and scam indicators
- Generate a scam risk score from 0–100

## 🧠 Rule-Based Scam Detection
The platform evaluates:
- Registration / processing fees
- Gmail or unofficial email domains
- Missing official websites
- Unrealistic salary offers
- WhatsApp-only communication
- Instant certificate guarantees
- Scam-related keywords

## 📊 Interactive Analytics Dashboard
- Weekly submission trends
- Risk distribution charts
- Scam keyword frequency tracking
- Activity heatmaps
- Submission management table

## 📈 Visual Risk Reports
- Pie charts
- Risk score progress bars
- Scam pattern breakdown
- Trust checklist indicators

## 📄 PDF Export (Prototype)
Generate downloadable internship analysis reports.

# 💡 Problem Statement

Many students fall victim to fake internship scams involving:
- Registration fee fraud
- Fake certificates
- Unrealistic placement promises
- WhatsApp-only recruiters
- Fake training programs

InternShield aims to create awareness and provide a quick verification system to help students stay safe.

---

# ⚙️ How It Works

The system calculates a risk score using multiple factors.

Example factors include:

| Risk Factor | Score Impact |
|-------------|--------------|
| Registration Fee | +30 |
| Gmail/Yahoo Email | +20 |
| No Website | +25 |
| WhatsApp Communication | +10 |
| Unrealistic Salary | +15 |
| Scam Keywords | +10 each |

The final score is categorized as:

- ✅ Safe (0–39)
- ⚠️ Suspicious (40–69)
- 🚨 High Risk (70–100)
